# RULES — governance/

> SOPs, controls, and audit checklists. Traceable, versioned, audit-ready.

---

## What belongs here

- Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
- Internal controls documentation.
- Audit checklists and compliance trackers.
- Quality assurance frameworks.
- Risk and incident logs (de-identified).

## What does not belong here

- Patient-level data or identifiers.
- Signed legal agreements (go in `legal/`).
- Financial models (go in `finance/`).
- Operational run files (go in `ops/`).

## Standards

- Every SOP must include: version number, effective date, and owner.
- Audit checklists must include: date, site, reviewer, and status.
- All documents must be audit-ready — clear, traceable, no ambiguity.
- Use de-identified examples only in any illustrations or case references.

## Naming convention

- Use `lowercase_with_underscores`.
- Add date prefix when relevant: `YYYY-MM-DD_name.ext`
- Include version in filename when iterating: `sop_name_v2.md`

## Before adding a file

- [ ] Document includes version, date, and owner.
- [ ] No PHI or patient identifiers included.
- [ ] Filename follows naming convention.
- [ ] Document is complete enough to be acted on independently.
