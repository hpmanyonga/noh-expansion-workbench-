## Voice and Tone

NOH speaks plainly, honestly, and directly. The voice is human — not corporate.

| Do                                      | Don't                                              |
|-----------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| Plain English, one idea per sentence    | Jargon, complex medical terminology                |
| Short, active sentences                 | Passive voice and filler phrases                   |
| State what you offer and what you don't | Overpromise or use vague superlatives              |
| Sound like a person                     | Sound like a press release                         |

---

## Writing Rules

1. **Use plain, direct language.** One idea per sentence. Cut jargon.
   - Use: "We offer Pap smears at our community sites."
   - Not: "Our facilities proudly provide cervical cytological screening services."

2. **Eliminate AI and buzzword phrases.** No clichés.
   - Use: "Our team-based maternity model improves continuity and lowers costs."
   - Not: "Let's dive into our transformative maternity care approach."

3. **Be direct. Be concise.** Remove filler.
   - Use: "Book your first visit online."
   - Not: "You may want to consider visiting our conveniently located facility."

4. **Sound human. Stay natural.** Starting with "And" or "But" is fine.
   - Use: "And we review outcomes weekly."

5. **Avoid marketing hype.** No 'revolutionary,' 'world-class,' or 'cutting-edge.'
   - Use: "This service helps detect cervical cancer earlier."

6. **Be honest. No overpromising.** State limits clearly.
   - Use: "We don't provide IVF. We can refer you."

7. **Cut fluff and filler.** Keep only what's useful.
   - Use: "We completed intake for 85 women this week."

8. **Use action-oriented language.** Start with verbs when possible.
   - Use: "Book online." "Submit your results."

9. **Structure for scanning.** Use bullets, headings, and short paragraphs. Always give next steps.

## Banned Words and Phrases

Never use in client-facing content:

| Banned                  | Use instead                          |
|-------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| dive in / let's dive    | start / begin / here's how           |
| cutting-edge            | effective / proven / reliable        |
| game-changer            | describe the actual impact           |
| transformative          | describe what changed and how        |
| revolutionary           | describe the specific improvement    |
| world-class             | state the actual quality metric      |
| innovative              | describe the specific approach       |
| synergy / leverage      | describe the actual relationship     |
| seamless                | describe the actual experience       |
| empower                 | describe the specific capability     |

## Formatting

- Use headings and subheadings to break up content.
- Use bullet lists for 3 or more items.
- Keep paragraphs to 3 sentences maximum.
- Always include a clear next step or call to action.
- Informal tone is acceptable in SMS and WhatsApp.
  - Example: `i'll check with the team and reply soon.`

## Claude Checklist

Before generating any client-facing content, confirm:

- [ ] Plain language — no jargon, no buzzwords
- [ ] No banned words or phrases
- [ ] Honest and accurate — no overpromising
- [ ] Structured with headings and bullets
- [ ] Includes a clear next step
- [ ] Colors, if used, match brand palette (`#599591`, `#f3bdc4`)
- [ ] Approved by user before saving to `outputs/`
